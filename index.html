The Crafty Creations Club

At The Crafty Creations Club our mission is to give back to our community by creating experiences that build your child's confidence. We do this by offering different art experiences that help your child grow their passion, learn how to express themselves and connect to their inner artist.

Our products

Kids Craft Kits

Each kit has an exciting opportunity to explore and learn about different science topics all while creating a work of art! Our kits include everything you need to take a project from start to finish while developing your child's artistic ability! Fun for all ages! (Young children may need adult assistance)

Sensory Bins

Have you heard about our seasonally themed Sensory Bins? They're sensational! Sensory Bins are perfect for toddlers developing their fine motor skills or anyone with sensory processing disorders. Each bin includes a "Scavenger Hunt" activity that helps make clean up fun!

About The Owner

Danielle has always had a passion for crafting. Even as a young child, she has preferred to keep her hands busy creating art. The concept of a kid's crafting club came after years of facilitating craft projects as a volunteer in her childrens' classrooms and as an educator in the local public schools. Danielle knows the importance of the impact art has on developing students. In an effort to help teachers, Danielle offers in-person crafting experiences for classrooms.
